About Me

MCO. Lanek Stephen Omuk

I am a dedicated Clinical Officer with over 5 years of experience in general medicine and diagnostic care. I currently practice at Omuksphere Medical and Diagnostic Centre, where I focus on delivering patient centered care grounded in evidence-based medicine.

My approach combines thorough clinical examination with advanced diagnostic tools to ensure every patient receives an accurate diagnosis and a clear, actionable treatment plan. I believe in empowering my patients with knowledge about their health.
